# User Manual: Signing Up for Leonardo.Ai and Adding an Emu to a Picture

This manual walks you through creating a Leonardo.Ai account and using the AI editor to add an emu into an existing picture.

---

## Part 1: Signing Up for app.leonardo.ai

### Step 1 — Open the Sign-Up Page
Go to **app.leonardo.ai**. You will see the sign-up screen with several options: **Canva**, **Apple**, **Google**, **Microsoft**, or **Continue with Email**.

![Sign-up options](images/01_signup_options.png)

### Step 2 — Continue with Email
Click **Continue with Email**. Enter your email address in the field provided (e.g. `example@gmail.com`), then click **Continue**.

![Enter email address](images/02_signup_email.png)

### Step 3 — Create Your Password
On the next screen, your email address will already be filled in. Create a password, then re-enter it in the **Enter Password Again** field. Click **Create Account** to finish.

![Create account password](images/03_create_password.png)

### Step 4 — Arrive at the Dashboard
Once signed up, you'll land on the Leonardo.Ai home page ("Yours to Create"). From here you can access **Image**, **Video**, **3D**, **Blueprints**, **Flow State**, **Upscaler**, and **Draw** tools from the left sidebar or the prompt bar at the top.

![Home dashboard](images/04_home_dashboard.png)

---

## Part 2: Adding an Emu to a Picture

### Step 1 — Start with Your Picture
Here is the original picture we'll be editing — a shepherd and his dog standing on a hillside overlooking farmland and the sea. Notice there is no emu in the scene yet.

![Initial picture before edit](images/00_initial_picture.png)

### Step 2 — Open the Image Tool and Enter a Prompt
From the top prompt bar, click the **image icon** and type your instruction, for example:

> *"add Emu to this picture."*

### Step 3 — Attach the Picture as a Reference
Click the image icon again to attach your picture. A **Reference** panel will appear, showing the **Image Reference** option, which uses the visual style and composition of an image to influence the output.

![Reference panel](images/06_final_result.png)

### Step 4 — Select the Media to Upload
Click **Image Reference** to open the **Select Media** window. Choose the **Your Uploads** tab, then select the picture you want to edit (or click **Upload an image** if it isn't uploaded yet). Click **Confirm** once selected.

![Select media dialog](images/05_select_media.png)

### Step 5 — Generate the Image
With your prompt ("add Emu to this picture") and reference image both set, click the **Generate** button in the top-right corner. Leonardo.Ai will process the request and produce a new version of your picture with the emu added.

### Step 6 — Review the Final Result
The final image now includes an emu standing in the field alongside the shepherd, the dog, and the grazing cows.

![Final result with emu added](images/06_final_result.png)
