1. Grading scale

Every course is assessed on a numeric scale from 0 to 100 points, which converts to one of seven letter grades:

2. GPA formula

The Grade Point Average is calculated as the credit-weighted mean of all completed courses (passed or failed), excluding courses currently in progress that have no final score yet:

GPA = Σ (credits × grade points) / Σ (credits)

3. Rounding

The resulting GPA is rounded to two decimal places.

4. Notes

  • Courses still in progress are not included until a final score is recorded.
  • Failed courses (FX, F) contribute 0 grade points but their credits are still counted, lowering the GPA.
  • Each course is counted once, using its most recent final grade — a retaken course's earlier failing attempt is shown for reference but excluded from the totals.
